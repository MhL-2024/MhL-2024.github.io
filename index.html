My research broadly explores river networks within the fields of geomorphology, hydrology, and biogeochemistry across regional to global scales. I investigate how tectonic forcing and climate jointly shape river network and drainage basin structures, as well as how watershed structures influence hydrological processes. Currently, my work aims to refine global greenhouse gas emission estimates from inland waters by incorporating river network dynamics into modeling frameworks.
